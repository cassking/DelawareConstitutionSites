The First State Celebrates Constitution Day 2007

Announcement September 16th, 2-3pm Constitution Day Program

Dear Colleagues:

This Sunday, September 16th, we will be having a Constitution Day program at the law school in which we will honoring a group of high school students who wrote essays for this year's Constitution Day Essay Collection. The program will be from 2:00 to 3:30 pm in the Ruby Vale Moot Courtroom. Judge Kent Jordan of the Third Circuit will be speaking. A reception will follow the event.

I know that all of you are busy and many do not live close to the school. So please do not feel any obligation to attend this event. Still, if any of you have the time and are willing to come, we would be honored by your presence. We think it will be a fun and inspiring program.

If you're planning to attend, please try to let Connie Sweeney know at cmsweeney@widener.edu.

Thank you,
Alan Garfield
